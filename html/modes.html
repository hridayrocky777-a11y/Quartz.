Study Modes

Quartz adapts to different learning environments with flexible study modes designed for both focused home learning and quick classroom access.

Home Mode provides a personalized AI-powered workspace where students can save chats, organize notes, track streaks, and access long-term learning tools through their account. Users are required to sign in to access and save their progress.

School Mode offers fast temporary access for students who need quick studying during class without creating an account or signing in. Warning: Closing the tab or refreshing the page will permanently erase temporary chats and study progress.
